Cheesecake Recipe

Ingredients

    1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs

    ⅓ cup butter, melted

    ¼ cup white sugar

    3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, room temperature

    Cup white sugar

    4 large eggs, room temperature

    1 tablespoon vanilla extract

    1 teaspoon cream of tartar

    1 pint sour cream

    ½ cup white sugar

    1 teaspoon vanilla extract

  1. Preheat the oven and prep the pan:

      2. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.

      Wrap the springform pan with one large piece of heavy-duty aluminum foil, covering the underside of the pan and extending all the way to the top.

      Repeat with another sheet of foil for insurance.

      Spray the inside of the pan with nonstick cooking spray.

  2. Make the graham cracker crust.

      3. In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, sugar, and salt.

      Stir until well-combined.

      Press the crumbs into an even layer on the bottom of the pan.

      Bake for 10 minutes until set.

      Remove the pan from the oven and set aside.

      Reduce the oven temperature to 325 degrees.

  3. Make the cheesecake batter.

      4. In the bowl of an elecric mixer, combine the cream cheese, sugar, and flour.

      Beat on medium speed until just smooth. Scrape the bottom and sides of the bowl to be sure the mixture is evenly combined.

      Add the vanilla, lemon zest, and salt; beat on low speed until just combined.

      Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing on low speed until incorporated.

      Mix in the sour cream.

      Make sure the batter is uniform but do not over-mix.

  4. Pour the batter on top of the crust.

      5. Bake!

      Set the unbaked cheesecake in a large roasting pan.

      Pour boiling water into the roasting pan until it comes about one inch up the side of the cake pan.

      Bake until the cake is just set. This should take one and a half to two hours.

      Cool!

      Carefully remove the roasting pan from the oven and set it on a wire rack.

      Cool the cheesecake in a water bath until the water is just warm. This should take about 45 minutes.

      Remove the springform pan from the water bath.

      Transfer the cheesecake to the refridgerator to cool for at least 8 hours or overnight.

  5. Enjoy~! You may serve the cheesecake plain, or with whichever garnish you may desire.
